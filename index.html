Breathing Wave Clock

Context: This sketch is designed for people who want to slow down or regulate their breathing during moments of stress, rest, or transition. It treats time as a rhythmic experience rather than something to be measured precisely.

  • Context: This sketch is intended for short breathing or mindfulness breaks, such as during studying, resting, or preparing for sleep.
  • Design decisions: I chose a smooth, continuous wave to mirror natural breathing patterns and used continuous time so the motion never jumps. A minimal visual style and soft color changes were used to avoid distraction and support calm focus.
  • Future work: Future versions could allow users to change breathing pace or switch between different breathing rhythms. Sound or subtle haptic feedback could also be added.

Continuous Shadow Clock

Context: This sketch is for users who want to perceive time in a more natural and intuitive way, similar to observing sunlight and shadows change throughout the day.

  • Context: The sketch can be used as an ambient time display for reflection, learning, or background awareness rather than precise scheduling.
  • Design decisions: I used a pole-and-shadow metaphor inspired by sundials and mapped time continuously to shadow angle and length to avoid abrupt jumps. Simple geometry and a restrained color palette keep the focus on motion rather than decoration.
  • Future work: This sketch could be extended by incorporating seasonal or geographic variation, or by allowing users to switch between abstract and realistic shadow behavior.

Candle Clock

Context:This sketch is designed for focused work, quiet reflection, or meditation, where time is experienced as something that is gradually consumed rather than counted.

  • Context: The candle clock can be used during short focus sessions, similar to a pomodoro timer, but with an emphasis on flow rather than precision.
  • Design decisions: I chose a candle metaphor because burning is irreversible, reinforcing the idea that time cannot be reclaimed. Each candle burns continuously for a fixed interval, then resets while incrementing a counter to show accumulated time. The visuals are intentionally minimal to reduce distraction.
  • Future work:Future improvements could include customizable burn durations, optional sound cues when a candle finishes, or grouping candles into longer work sessions.
